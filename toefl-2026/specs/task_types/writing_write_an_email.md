# Write an Email

**Section**: Writing
**CEFR Range**: A2–C2 (communicative writing)

## Construct

Measures the ability to produce a multisentence written text that achieves a specific communicative purpose in academic or social settings. Tests elaboration, syntactic variety, social conventions, and accuracy.

## Format

- **Stimulus**: A scenario presented in text regarding either an academic or social setting, with visual graphics providing context
- **Task**: Test taker writes an email for a specific communicative purpose, such as:
  - Making a recommendation
  - Extending an invitation
  - Proposing a solution to a problem
- **Questions**: 1 written response per test form

## Scoring

- Scored 0–5 by AI engine + human raters
- See `rubrics/writing_email_rubric.md` for full rubric

## Scoring Dimensions & AI Features

| Dimension | Feature Examples |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Content ("elaboration … supports the communication purpose") | Number of sentences, discourse coherence, similarity to question prompt |
| Syntactic/Lexical variety ("syntactic variety … idiomatic word choice") | Sentence variety, word frequency, correctness of collocations |
| Social Conventions ("politeness, register, organization … formulation of actions") | Use of politeness indicators (modals, hedge words) |
| Accuracy/Errors ("Almost no lexical or grammatical errors") | Grammaticality, grammatical errors, word/usage errors, mechanical errors |

## Constraints

- Scenario must be clearly described with sufficient context
- Communicative purpose must be specific and actionable
- Both academic and social settings are valid
- Visual graphics should provide supporting context

## Skills Assessed

- Achieve the designated communication goal, following basic social conventions
- Produce adequately elaborated, clear, and cohesive text
- Make accurate and appropriate use of a range of grammatical structures and vocabulary
- Follow mechanical conventions of English (spelling, punctuation, capitalization)


## 3. Interaction & Delivery Mechanics
### Delivery Sequence & States
1. **Split-Screen State**: The email prompt is displayed on the left, and the text input area is on the right.
2. **Writing Area Rules**: Strictly plain-text input. No formatting toolbar.
3. **Copy/Paste Restrictions**: Plagiarism prevention is active. Copying from the prompt to the response area is blocked.
4. **Word Count**: A live word counter tracks the input length.
5. **Navigation**: Forward-only navigation.

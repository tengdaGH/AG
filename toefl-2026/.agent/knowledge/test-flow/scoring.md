## Section Scoring Methods

### Reading & Listening (Machine-scored)
- Each item: 1 point (correct) or 0 (incorrect)
- Raw score: 0–35
- Conversion: IRT true score equating → 1–6 band
- Difficulty path affects scaled score

### Writing
| Task | Raw Score | Method |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Build a Sentence (×10) | 0–10 | Key-scored (exact match) |
| Write an Email (×1) | 0–5 | AI engine + human rater |
| Academic Discussion (×1) | 0–5 | AI engine + human rater |
| **Total** | **0–20** | Weighted equipercentile → 1–6 band |

**Writing scoring features**: content elaboration, syntactic variety, social conventions, lexical accuracy

### Speaking
| Task | Raw Score | Method |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Listen and Repeat (×7) | 0–5 | AI engine |
| Take an Interview (×4) | 0–5 | AI engine |
| **Total** | **0–55** | Weighted equipercentile → 1–6 band |

**Speaking scoring features**: fluency, intelligibility, repeat accuracy, vocabulary/grammar, organization

## Overall Score
- Average of 4 section band scores, rounded to nearest 0.5
- Example: sections 4.5, 5.0, 4.0, 5.5 → average 4.75 → Overall **5.0**
